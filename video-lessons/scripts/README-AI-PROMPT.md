## üöÄ How to Use

### Step 1: Choose Your Version
- **Most AI tools**: Use `lesson-01-AI-PROMPT.txt` (no pauses)
- **Check your tool's documentation** - if it supports pauses, use the "with-pauses" version

### Step 2: Copy the Prompt
1. Open the appropriate file
2. Select all text (Ctrl+A or Cmd+A)
3. Copy (Ctrl+C or Cmd+C)

### Step 3: Paste into AI Tool
1. Go to your AI video tool (Vidnoz, HeyGen, etc.)
2. Paste the entire prompt into the script/text input field
3. Select your settings:
   - **Voice**: Clear, professional English (American or British)
   - **Speed**: Slightly slower than normal (120-140 words per minute)
   - **Avatar**: Professional-looking, business casual
   - **Background**: Clean, professional (office or neutral)

### Step 4: Generate Video
1. Review your settings
2. Click "Generate" or "Create Video"
3. Wait for processing (2-5 minutes for ~6 minute video)
4. Download in highest quality (1080p)
